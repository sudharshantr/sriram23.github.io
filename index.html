About Us

CoderDojo has spread across the World with massive result .It makes children to learn coding in a easy way. Many children are benefited through this CoderDojo. Now there are nearly 700 centers across world. In India there are 10 centers, And we are the second in Tamil Nadu.

First time in Coimbatore: Coimbatore CoderDojo is a free computer programming club for children. It is a Part of the world movement. We teach how to code, develop websites, apps, programs, games and explore technology in a formal and creative environment. In addition to learning code, attendees meet like minded people and are exposed to the possibilities of technology.

As now we are officially registered on CoderDojo and we are very eager and curious to launch the main event, We are a team of very skillful persons who are very interested in sharing their valuable skills. We have planned to make the event very useful, we have planned to provide resources for various different platforms for instance from Scratch to Python. Hence we are up and active.
